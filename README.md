# NO-BULLSHIT-PAIN-TRACKER

A privacy-first, evidence-disciplined project for recording chronic-pain care,
treatment changes, functional outcomes, adverse events, and healthcare-system
friction without turning one patient's experience into an unsupported universal
claim.

> **Research prototype — not medical or legal advice.** Do not change, start, or
> stop medication based on this repository. Abrupt changes to dependence-forming
> medicines can be dangerous; seek qualified clinical help. If someone may be in
> immediate danger or at risk of self-harm, contact local emergency or crisis
> services now.

## Research principles

1. Patient reports are evidence of reported experience, not proof of causation.
2. Clinical records, guidelines, laws, and interpretations are different evidence
   classes and must not be conflated.
3. No raw health records or identifying information belong in this public repo.
4. Every system-failure allegation needs a dated event, jurisdiction, source type,
   uncertainty statement, and opportunity for alternative explanations.
5. Guidelines inform care but do not automatically establish an individual legal
   entitlement, negligence, misconduct, or a required prescription.
6. Research may criticize institutions, but the data model must be capable of
   recording competent care, uncertainty, and evidence against the hypothesis too.

## Status

The first source document has been triaged. It is an AI-generated clinical/legal
brief about a person with complex chronic pain and a proposed opioid taper. The
source is retained outside this public repository. Its medical history is treated
as patient-reported unless corroborated by primary records. Its legal conclusions
are not accepted as legal determinations.
